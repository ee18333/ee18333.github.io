W7: Data story

Dawn Holland from NIESR believes that the rise in inflation is a temporary phenomenon because the rise in energy price is transitory. However, the chart shows that the sharp increase in energy price around April 2021 intersects at where general price starts to increase. Since then, energy price keeps increasing persistently, with little signs of coming back down. The continuous increase in energy price has created an inflationary environment that could be lasting.
